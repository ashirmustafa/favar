Deleting your account

We're sorry to see you go but if you are sure that you
want to delete your account, please submit your email
address used to create your Favarr account.

When you submit your email, our support team will reach out to you to confirm
your identity. Once your identity is verified, the account deletion process will take
up to 5 business days. When your account is successfully deleted, we will inform
you via an email.

Once the process is completed, you will be logged out of Favarr and will not be
able to use your account.
